# Voice entry Inventory home foods, hands-free entry.
Voice entry for your home foods (pantry) Inventory.  Started working on this March 11, 2026.  

Sling your phone around your neck.  It will be your microphone.  Uses your home desktop and a smartphone microphone and your home sifi to connect them.  Internet for AI connection.  I hope to make that optional, but no promise.

Could this project fall by the wayside?  Sure could, and one of the reasons it might is that the money for my internet connection really is vapor dollars.  $180/month that is way higher than my income.
